How It Works

Three steps to financial freedom

Our streamlined process is designed to get you relief as quickly as possible, with full transparency at every stage.

01

Apply & Get Assessed

Fill out our 4-step application in under 10 minutes. We review your loan details, income, and hardship situation to determine eligibility.

→
02

Pay Your Approved Amount

Once approved, you only need to pay as low as 5% of your loan balance upfront. We pay off your full loan immediately, and the remaining balance becomes a simple installment plan at just 1% interest — based on what you can comfortably afford monthly.

→
03

We Pay It Off Instantly

HappyMovement USA pays your lender in full right away. You then repay us on your own schedule with a low 1% interest installment plan. No pressure, no hidden fees — just a fresh start.

Qualification

Do you qualify?

Most borrowers facing financial hardship are eligible. Here's what we look for.

Eligibility Requirements

✓
Outstanding loan balance of $10,000 or more (personal, student, or auto loans)
✓
Demonstrable financial hardship — job loss, medical bills, divorce, or significant income reduction
✓
U.S. resident with a valid Social Security Number or ITIN
✓
Loan must not be in active bankruptcy proceedings
✓
Mortgage loans and business loans are eligible
✗
Government-backed SBA loans are not eligible
✗
Loans already enrolled in another relief program
